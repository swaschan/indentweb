Indent

For writers, by writers.

Indent is a set of tools for writers, game designers, and roleplayers to create magnificent universes and everything within them.

Characters

After all, every story needs a character or two..

Make 'Em

Indent allows you to make your characters with minimal fuss, allowing full control over it. Be it just adding in the nitty-gritty details that matter - oh - so much, or redefining your entire character, Indent does it all!

Crank 'Em

Indent makes it easy to assign Objects [via each character's personal Inventory], or magic spells, or pretty much anything you wish to your character using Nodes! Oh, and did I mention that you can dynamically change or add/remove these as your story progresses?

Track 'Em

Perhaps the coolest feature of all, an interactive map displayed right in your browser, containing the journey of your character over time. Oh, and you can also see the progress of other characters in your masterpiece. Wicked.
